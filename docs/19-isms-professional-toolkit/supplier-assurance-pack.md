# Supplier Assurance Pack

Supplier assurance ensures external providers are assessed, contracted, monitored, and offboarded based on risk.

## Pack contents

- supplier tiering model
- supplier due diligence questionnaire
- supplier risk rating matrix
- cloud shared responsibility matrix
- contract clause checklist
- subprocessor review checklist
- annual supplier review template
- supplier exit checklist

## Supplier tiering example

| Tier | Description | Review frequency |
|---|---|---|
| Tier 1 | Critical supplier handling sensitive data or critical services | Annual or more frequent |
| Tier 2 | Important supplier with moderate security relevance | Every 1–2 years |
| Tier 3 | Low-risk supplier | Lightweight review |

## Due diligence topics

- security governance
- certifications and audit reports
- access control
- encryption
- incident notification
- vulnerability management
- business continuity
- data location and subprocessors
- privacy controls
- termination and data deletion

## Evidence

- supplier register
- risk rating
- due diligence results
- contract security clauses
- review records
- issue tracker
