# Glossary

## Asset

Anything of value to the organization or that supports something of value.

## Control

A measure that modifies risk.

## Information Security Management System

A coordinated management system used to establish, implement, maintain, and continually improve information security.

## Inherent risk

Risk before additional treatment under consideration.

## Residual risk

Risk remaining after treatment.

## Risk owner

Person or role with authority and accountability to manage and accept a risk.

## Statement of Applicability

Document explaining Annex A control applicability, justification, implementation status, and related evidence.

## Vulnerability

A weakness or condition that may be exploited by a threat or contribute to an unwanted event.
