## Usage guidance

The same letters can have different meanings. For example, **CI** can mean *configuration item* in service management and *continuous integration* in software delivery, while **IP** can mean *intellectual property* or *Internet Protocol*. Write the intended full form at first use whenever context does not make the meaning unambiguous.

Organization and framework names such as COBIT, ISACA, and ITIL are retained in their current common form. Their historical expansions are included to help readers, but the expansion should not be used to imply that it remains the organization's or framework owner's formal name.
