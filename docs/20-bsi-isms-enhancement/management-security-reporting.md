# Management Security Reporting

Management reports should help leadership understand security posture, risks, resource needs, and required decisions.

## Report objectives

A good report should answer:

- What is our current security status?
- What changed since the last report?
- Which risks require management attention?
- Are controls effective?
- Are resources sufficient?
- What decisions are needed?
- Which improvements are recommended?

## Recommended report structure

1. Executive summary
2. Risk posture
3. Current threat situation
4. Security objective progress
5. Major incidents and lessons learned
6. Control assurance results
7. Audit findings and corrective actions
8. Supplier and cloud risk status
9. Resource and budget needs
10. Prioritized recommendations
11. Decisions required
12. Action log

## Prioritized recommendation format

| Recommendation | Risk addressed | Benefit | Effort | Owner | Decision needed |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|

## Best practices

- Prioritize clearly.
- Estimate effort realistically.
- Distinguish urgent risks from long-term improvements.
- Include management decisions and residual-risk handling.
- Archive reports and decisions in a controlled manner.

## Evidence

- management reports
- decision logs
- action trackers
- key performance indicator (KPI)/key risk indicator (KRI) dashboard
- management review minutes

## ISO requirement, implementation guidance, and best practice

- **ISO requirement:** This chapter explains **Management Security Reporting** without reproducing standard text. Determine formal obligations from the applicable clauses, scope, risk treatment, Statement of Applicability, and binding legal or contractual requirements.
- **Implementation guidance:** Adapt the described roles, frequency, workflow, and evidence to the organization.
- **Best practice:** Enhancements are optional unless adopted through policy, contract, or risk treatment.

## Practical example

An organization uses this guidance to strengthen an existing ISO/IEC 27001 process with a clearly assigned security role, direct escalation, and practical operating records suited to its governance model.
