# Information Security Officer Role Profile

The Information Security Officer, ISB, or ISMS Manager coordinates the information security process and supports leadership in operating the ISMS.

## Mission

The role promotes, coordinates, and monitors information security across the organization.

## Typical responsibilities

- coordinate ISMS implementation and operation
- support risk assessment and risk treatment
- prepare management reports
- coordinate policy framework
- advise projects and major changes
- monitor control implementation
- coordinate awareness activities
- support incident management
- maintain SoA and ISMS documentation
- coordinate internal audits and corrective actions
- report significant risks to leadership

## Required authority

The role should have:

- access to relevant information
- ability to escalate risks
- right to be involved in major projects
- direct reporting opportunity to top management when needed
- authority to coordinate control owners

## Competence

Useful competence areas:

- ISO/IEC 27001
- risk management
- governance and compliance
- technical security fundamentals
- communication and facilitation
- audit preparation
- incident management
- supplier and cloud security

## Independence considerations

The role should avoid conflicts where the same person both owns and independently assures the same control without compensating review.
