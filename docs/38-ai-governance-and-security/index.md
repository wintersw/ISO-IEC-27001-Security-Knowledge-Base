## ISO/IEC 27001 relationship

This topic supports the ISMS by strengthening risk-based implementation of relevant clauses:

- Clause 4 — AI systems and data as part of the organisation's context and interested-party expectations
- Clause 6 — AI-specific risk assessment, risk treatment, and security objectives
- Clause 7 — competence and awareness for AI governance; documented information about AI systems
- Clause 8 — operational planning and control including AI system intake, assessment, and monitoring
- Clause 9 — monitoring, measurement, audit, and management review of AI-related controls
- Clause 10 — corrective action and continual improvement of AI governance practices

Relevant Annex A controls are referenced in the implementation and evidence sections of each page.
