# Integrated Management System Checklist

- [ ] existing management systems identified
- [ ] shared processes identified
- [ ] shared evidence identified
- [ ] conflicting processes identified
- [ ] document control alignment reviewed
- [ ] audit program alignment reviewed
- [ ] corrective action process alignment reviewed
- [ ] management review integration considered

## Evidence

Retain the completed checklist with reviewer, date, decision, and follow-up actions.

## How to use this checklist

Use this checklist for **Integrated Management System**. Assign a reviewer, scope and deadline, and evidence for each answer. Failed items require an owned action, exception, or risk decision; a checked box alone is not evidence.

## Practical example

Before approving **Integrated Management System**, the reviewer checks an authoritative source, assigns a failed item for remediation, and signs off only after resolution or formal acceptance is evidenced.

## ISO requirement, implementation guidance, and best practice

This checklist is guidance, not prescribed ISO wording. Formal requirements depend on the scoped outcome supported by **Integrated Management System**. Evidence retention and independent review of high-risk failures are good practice.
