# Surveillance Audit Preparation Checklist

- [ ] review previous audit findings
- [ ] update risk register
- [ ] confirm Statement of Applicability (SoA) accuracy
- [ ] collect recent operating evidence
- [ ] review management review actions
- [ ] test selected controls
- [ ] prepare control owners
- [ ] confirm corrective action status

## Evidence

Retain the completed checklist, supporting records, decisions, and assigned actions.

## How to use this checklist

Use this checklist for **Surveillance Audit Preparation**. Assign a reviewer, scope and deadline, and evidence for each answer. Failed items require an owned action, exception, or risk decision; a checked box alone is not evidence.

## Practical example

Before approving **Surveillance Audit Preparation**, the reviewer checks an authoritative source, assigns a failed item for remediation, and signs off only after resolution or formal acceptance is evidenced.

## ISO requirement, implementation guidance, and best practice

This checklist is guidance, not prescribed ISO wording. Formal requirements depend on the scoped outcome supported by **Surveillance Audit Preparation**. Evidence retention and independent review of high-risk failures are good practice.
