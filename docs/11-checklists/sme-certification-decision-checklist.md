# SME Certification Decision Checklist

- [ ] customer requirement exists
- [ ] scope is realistic
- [ ] owners assigned
- [ ] risk process operating
- [ ] evidence available
- [ ] management committed
- [ ] cost and effort understood
- [ ] certification timing justified

## Evidence

Retain the completed checklist, reviewer, date, decisions, and follow-up actions.
