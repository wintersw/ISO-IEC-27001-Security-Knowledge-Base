# Stage 1 Readiness Checklist

- [ ] scope statement approved
- [ ] context and interested parties documented
- [ ] risk methodology approved
- [ ] risk assessment performed
- [ ] SoA prepared
- [ ] core policies approved
- [ ] internal audit planned
- [ ] management review planned or completed
- [ ] document control established

## Evidence

Retain the completed checklist, supporting records, decisions, and assigned actions.
