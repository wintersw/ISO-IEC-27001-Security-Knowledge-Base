# Management Review Evidence Checklist

Use this as an evidence-quality companion to the operational [management review checklist](./management-review.md), linking both to the same authoritative review record.

- [ ] agenda retained
- [ ] participants recorded
- [ ] required inputs covered
- [ ] risk status reviewed
- [ ] objectives reviewed
- [ ] audit results reviewed
- [ ] incidents reviewed
- [ ] resource needs reviewed
- [ ] decisions recorded
- [ ] actions assigned owners and due dates

## Evidence

Retain the completed checklist with reviewer, date, decision, and follow-up actions.

## How to use this checklist

Use this checklist for **Management Review Evidence**. Assign a reviewer, scope and deadline, and evidence for each answer. Failed items require an owned action, exception, or risk decision; a checked box alone is not evidence.

## Practical example

Before approving **Management Review Evidence**, the reviewer checks an authoritative source, assigns a failed item for remediation, and signs off only after resolution or formal acceptance is evidenced.

## ISO requirement, implementation guidance, and best practice

This checklist is guidance, not prescribed ISO wording. Formal requirements depend on the scoped outcome supported by **Management Review Evidence**. Evidence retention and independent review of high-risk failures are good practice.
