# Continual Improvement Audit Evidence Checklist

- [ ] improvement register exists and is current
- [ ] improvement sources are recorded
- [ ] items are linked to risks, controls, findings, or incidents
- [ ] owners and due dates are assigned
- [ ] high-priority overdue items are escalated
- [ ] corrective actions include root-cause analysis
- [ ] effectiveness reviews are completed
- [ ] management review includes improvement status
- [ ] SoA and risk register are updated where needed
- [ ] repeat findings are analyzed

## Evidence

Retain the completed checklist with reviewer, date, decision, and follow-up actions.
