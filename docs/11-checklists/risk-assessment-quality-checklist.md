# Risk Assessment Quality Checklist

- [ ] risk scenarios are specific and understandable
- [ ] asset or process owner is identified
- [ ] risk owner is accountable
- [ ] impact and likelihood criteria are applied consistently
- [ ] existing controls are listed
- [ ] treatment option is selected
- [ ] residual risk is evaluated
- [ ] risk acceptance is approved where needed
- [ ] treatment actions have owners and due dates
- [ ] risk is linked to SoA where relevant

## Evidence

Retain the completed checklist with reviewer, date, decision, and follow-up actions.
