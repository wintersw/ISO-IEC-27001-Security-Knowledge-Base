# IT Governance ISMS Review Checklist
- [ ] security objectives linked to business objectives
- [ ] major IT risks visible to governance
- [ ] residual risk decisions documented
- [ ] resource constraints escalated
- [ ] security metrics reviewed
- [ ] supplier risk reviewed
- [ ] technical debt reviewed
- [ ] management decisions tracked

## Evidence

Retain the completed checklist with reviewer, date, decisions, and follow-up actions.

## How to use this checklist

Use this checklist for **IT Governance ISMS Review**. Assign a reviewer, scope and deadline, and evidence for each answer. Failed items require an owned action, exception, or risk decision; a checked box alone is not evidence.

## Practical example

Before approving **IT Governance ISMS Review**, the reviewer checks an authoritative source, assigns a failed item for remediation, and signs off only after resolution or formal acceptance is evidenced.

## ISO requirement, implementation guidance, and best practice

This checklist is guidance, not prescribed ISO wording. Formal requirements depend on the scoped outcome supported by **IT Governance ISMS Review**. Evidence retention and independent review of high-risk failures are good practice.
