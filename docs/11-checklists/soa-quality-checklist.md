# Statement of Applicability Quality Checklist

- [ ] all Annex A controls are represented
- [ ] applicability is marked for each control
- [ ] inclusion justifications are risk-based or requirement-based
- [ ] exclusion justifications are documented
- [ ] implementation status is current
- [ ] control owners are assigned
- [ ] evidence references are included
- [ ] related risks are linked
- [ ] SoA is approved
- [ ] SoA is reviewed after risk or scope changes

## Evidence

Retain the completed checklist with reviewer, date, decision, and follow-up actions.

## How to use this checklist

Use this checklist for **Statement of Applicability Quality**. Assign a reviewer, scope and deadline, and evidence for each answer. Failed items require an owned action, exception, or risk decision; a checked box alone is not evidence.

## Practical example

Before approving **Statement of Applicability Quality**, the reviewer checks an authoritative source, assigns a failed item for remediation, and signs off only after resolution or formal acceptance is evidenced.

## ISO requirement, implementation guidance, and best practice

This checklist is guidance, not prescribed ISO wording. Formal requirements depend on the scoped outcome supported by **Statement of Applicability Quality**. Evidence retention and independent review of high-risk failures are good practice.
