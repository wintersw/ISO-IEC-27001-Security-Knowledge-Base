# Cybersecurity and Enterprise Risk Integration

Cybersecurity risk is business risk. Integration with enterprise risk management prevents cyber risks from being isolated in technical registers that executives never use.

## Integration points

- shared impact language
- common risk appetite
- executive risk ownership
- business-service criticality
- financial and operational scenario analysis
- strategic project review
- supplier concentration risk
- insurance decisions
- board reporting

## Example

A ransomware scenario should not be described only as “malware infection.” Enterprise risk framing includes service outage, revenue loss, customer impact, contractual penalties, recovery cost, and reputational damage.

## Best practices

- Use business scenarios, not only technical vulnerabilities.
- Link cyber risks to strategic objectives and critical services.
- Avoid duplicate risk registers with inconsistent ratings.
- Include cyber scenarios in enterprise stress testing.
- Track cross-functional dependencies such as legal, communications, privacy, operations, and suppliers.

## ISO requirement, implementation guidance, and best practice

- **ISO requirement:** This chapter explains **Cybersecurity and Enterprise Risk Integration** without reproducing standard text. Determine formal obligations from the applicable clauses, scope, risk treatment, Statement of Applicability, and binding legal or contractual requirements.
- **Implementation guidance:** Adapt the described roles, frequency, workflow, and evidence to the organization.
- **Best practice:** Enhancements are optional unless adopted through policy, contract, or risk treatment.

## Evidence to retain

Retain records showing both design decisions and actual operation, such as:

- approved strategy or governance decision
- authority and accountability record
- risk and performance reporting
- oversight minutes and tracked actions

Intent documents are insufficient on their own; retain scoped operating records, approvals, exceptions, and verified follow-up.
