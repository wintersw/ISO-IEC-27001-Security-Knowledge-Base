# Oversight and Assurance

Oversight answers a critical question: how does leadership know that security activities are working?

## Assurance sources

- internal audit
- external audit
- control testing
- self-assessment
- vulnerability and penetration testing
- incident analysis
- supplier assurance
- metrics and dashboards
- management review
- customer or regulator feedback

## Example

A dashboard reports 100% completion of quarterly access reviews. Assurance asks whether the reviews covered the complete population, whether removals were completed, and whether managers had enough information to make meaningful decisions. Completion alone does not prove effectiveness.

## Best practices

- Combine management assurance and independent assurance.
- Sample operating evidence, not only policy documents.
- Report repeat findings as governance issues.
- Include effectiveness reviews after corrective actions.
- Escalate missing evidence and weak ownership.
