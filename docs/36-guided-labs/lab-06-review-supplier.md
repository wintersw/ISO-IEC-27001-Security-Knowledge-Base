# Lab 6 — Review a Critical Supplier

## Scenario

A cloud payroll provider processes employee identity, salary, bank, tax, and absence data. It uses subprocessors in several countries. The contract renews in ninety days.

## Tasks

1. Assign supplier risk tier.
2. Identify due-diligence questions.
3. Identify contract requirements.
4. Define evidence.
5. Identify operational monitoring.
6. Define exit requirements.

## Suggested focus areas

- access control and privileged support
- encryption
- incident notification
- continuity and recovery
- subprocessor governance
- data location
- deletion
- audit cooperation
- vulnerability management
- identity federation
- change notification

## Suggested decision

Approval with conditions may be appropriate if the provider has adequate controls but the contract lacks a clear subprocessor-notification period and deletion certificate. Conditions should have owners and renewal deadlines.

## Review questions

- Does certification cover the actual service?
- Are fourth parties visible?
- Can the organization exit safely?
- Is supplier evidence linked to the risk register?
