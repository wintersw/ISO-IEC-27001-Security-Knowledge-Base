## 7. Findings and follow-up rules

Define finding categories, escalation thresholds, report recipients, response deadlines, action ownership, target dates, evidence expected for closure, and who will independently verify effectiveness. Do not let the auditor close a finding solely on the basis of the finding owner's assertion.

## Usage guidance

Use this template to document **Internal Audit Plan**.

## Practical example

For one in-scope service, the owner completes the **Internal Audit Plan** record, links authoritative evidence, obtains review, and tracks rejected or incomplete items to closure.

## ISO requirement, implementation guidance, and best practice

This exact template is not an ISO requirement; it is guidance for recording **Internal Audit Plan** consistently. Controlled values, named owners, timestamps, approvals, and authoritative evidence improve assurance.
