# ISMS Investment Case Template

> Based in part on the return-on-investment document in the [ISO 27001:2022 Toolkit](https://github.com/PehanIn/ISO-27001-2022-Toolkit), copyright (c) 2024 Pehan Gunasekara, MIT License. The calculation, uncertainty treatment, and standards claims have been corrected and expanded.

This business-case aid is optional; ISO/IEC 27001 does not require a return-on-investment document. Use it to compare credible options and support resource decisions without presenting avoided loss or certification benefits as guaranteed cash returns.

Keep intangible benefits—such as trust, governance quality, and resilience—visible, but do not assign arbitrary monetary values merely to improve the result.

## Calculations

- **Net benefit** = total attributable benefits - total costs.
- **Simple ROI (%)** = (total attributable benefits - total costs) / total costs x 100.
- **Payback period** = time until cumulative attributable benefits equal cumulative costs.

For multi-year or material investments, use discounted cash flow and show the discount rate, timing assumptions, low/base/high scenarios, and sensitivity to the most uncertain inputs. Avoid double-counting one benefit under several labels.
