## Treatment status definitions

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Not started | Action approved but not started |
| In progress | Work is underway |
| Blocked | Action needs decision or resource |
| Complete | Action implemented and evidenced |
| Cancelled | Action no longer required and justified |

## Review

Treatment progress should be reviewed monthly or according to risk severity.
