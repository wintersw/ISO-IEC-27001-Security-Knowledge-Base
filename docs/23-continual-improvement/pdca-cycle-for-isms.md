# PDCA Cycle for ISMS

Plan-Do-Check-Act is a useful mental model for operating and improving an ISMS.

## Plan

Define the ISMS context, scope, policy, objectives, risk methodology, risk assessment, treatment plan, and selected controls.

Related chapters:

- [Clause 4 — Context](../03-iso27001/clause-4-context.md)
- [Clause 6 — Planning](../03-iso27001/clause-6-planning.md)
- [Risk Assessment Methodology](../05-risk-management/risk-methodology.md)
- [Statement of Applicability](../05-risk-management/statement-of-applicability.md)

## Do

Implement and operate the ISMS, controls, treatment actions, policies, procedures, and evidence processes.

Related chapters:

- [Clause 8 — Operation](../03-iso27001/clause-8-operation.md)
- [Control Lifecycle Management](../04-isms/control-lifecycle.md)
- [ISMS Documentation Template Pack](../22-isms-documentation-template-pack/index.md)

## Check

Monitor, measure, audit, test controls, review incidents, review suppliers, and evaluate performance.

Related chapters:

- [Clause 9 — Performance Evaluation](../03-iso27001/clause-9-performance-evaluation.md)
- [Internal Audit Program](../19-isms-professional-toolkit/internal-audit-program.md)
- [Control Assurance Methodology](../19-isms-professional-toolkit/control-assurance-methodology.md)
- [ISMS Health Dashboard](../19-isms-professional-toolkit/isms-health-dashboard.md)

## Act

Correct nonconformities, improve weak controls, update the risk register and SoA, allocate resources, and revise objectives.

Related chapters:

- [Clause 10 — Improvement](../03-iso27001/clause-10-improvement.md)
- [Nonconformity and Corrective Action](../08-auditing/nonconformity-corrective-action.md)
- [Management Review Pack](../19-isms-professional-toolkit/management-review-pack.md)

## Practical operating rhythm

| Cycle | Example activity |
|---|---|
| Weekly | Review critical incidents, vulnerabilities, urgent risks |
| Monthly | Review improvement backlog and overdue actions |
| Quarterly | Review controls, access, suppliers, objectives |
| Semi-annually | Run tabletop exercises and selected audits |
| Annually | Review context, scope, methodology, SoA, management review, audit program |
