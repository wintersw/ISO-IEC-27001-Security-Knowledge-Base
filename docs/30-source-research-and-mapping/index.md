# Research and Topic Mapping

This section shows how major security and privacy topics connect to the knowledge base, ISO/IEC 27001, and Annex A. Maintainers can use it to find coverage gaps, validate new material against authoritative references, and avoid creating disconnected or duplicate guidance.

## Rules used

- do not copy source text
- do not reproduce proprietary tables or diagrams
- use sources to identify themes and gaps
- write original, implementation-oriented content
- map new content to ISO/IEC 27001 and Annex A
- add templates and checklists where content should become operational
- link to existing pages instead of duplicating them
