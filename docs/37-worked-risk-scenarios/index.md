# Worked Risk Scenarios

This library provides reusable examples for risk workshops, project reviews, internal audit, management review, and training.

Each scenario follows a consistent structure:

- risk statement
- assets and business context
- threat and vulnerability
- potential impact
- existing controls
- treatment options
- residual-risk decision
- evidence
- monitoring indicators
- review triggers

The scenarios must be tailored before use. They are examples, not pre-approved risk ratings.
