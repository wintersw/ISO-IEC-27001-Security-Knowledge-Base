# Hands-On Labs

Labs turn reading into practice. Each short lab produces an artifact that can be reviewed against the guidance elsewhere in this knowledge base.

- [Build an asset register](build-asset-register.md) — identify assets, ownership, classification, and dependencies.
- [Perform a basic risk assessment](basic-risk-assessment.md) — describe a risk scenario, score it consistently, and select a response.

Use fictional or sanitized information in training environments. A facilitator should agree the scenario and assessment method before the exercise, then review whether the output is complete, internally consistent, and actionable.
